Inspired by Voyager

The Smyrna Stewardship project draws inspiration from the 1977 Voyager spacecraft mission — a time capsule of humanity’s best intentions, still traveling through space. We believe our local efforts, though small, are part of that same mission: to build a future worth inheriting.

“We are attempting to survive our time so we may live into yours.”
– Jimmy Carter, Voyager Golden Record Message
